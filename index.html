我每年有若干名研究生名额， 欢迎有意从事相关领域的各年级本科生、研究生加入我的团队！

I am an associate professor at the School of Computer Software, College of Intelligence and Computing, Tianjin University. I received my Ph.D. degree from Tianjin University in 2012. As a joint Ph.D., I studied at University of Alberta under China scholarship council scholarship program during 2009-2010. My research interests include image synthesis, computer vision and multimodal fusion, especially in the fields of culture heritage and municipal engineering. I am Associate Editor of Journal of Electronic Imaging, I also serve(d) for many conferences and journals including Signal Processing, JCLEPRO, Signal Processing Letter, Journal of Computer Applications, SIVP.

I have several master positions available in my lab now. If you are excited about computer vision, image synthesis and machine learning, then I am interested to hearing from you.
